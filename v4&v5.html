Hello, baby names!

For our project we will analyze a dataset of baby names with popularity over time and state in the US. We will create five different interactive visualizations that allow users to explore the name dataset from many different ways across many types of data. You can have an overview of one state. You can also click on nava button to explore more for name distribution by states or different years. Finally, we also found some interesting connection between name popularity and special events happened in a specific year, for example, the huge popularity drop with name 'Halley' and the Halley Comet in 1911 and name patterns during elections.
